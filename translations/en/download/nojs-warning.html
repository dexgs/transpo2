JavaScript is not available in your browser. This means decryption will be handled on the server instead of in your browser. The key to decrypt the download will only be stored by the server for the duration of the decryption process, but be aware that this is less secure than decrypting in your browser which requires JavaScript.

If you wish to proceed anyway, copy the decryption key from the address bar into the "Decryption key" field. The key is the segment of the URL which comes AFTER the # symbol.
